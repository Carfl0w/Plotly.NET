FSharp.Plotly

FSharp.Plotly: Histogram2d

Summary: This example shows how to create a one-dimensional histogram of a data samples in F#.

A histogram consisting of rectangles whose area is proportional to the frequency of a variable and whose width is equal to the class interval. The histogram chart represents the distribution of numerical data and can be created using the Chart.Histogram.

open FSharp.Plotly 

let rnd = System.Random()
let x = [for i=0 to 500 do yield rnd.NextDouble() ]

|> Chart.withSize(500.,500.)
