Plotly.NET: Candlestick Charts

Summary: This example shows how to create candlestick charts in F#.

A candlestick chart is useful for plotting stock prices over time. A candle is a group of high, open, close and low values over a period of time, e.g. 1 minute, 5 minute, hour, day, etc.. The x-axis is usually dateime values and y is a sequence of candle structures.
