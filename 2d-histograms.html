FSharp.Plotly: Histogram2d

Summary: This example shows how to create pie and doughnut charts in F#.

A pie or a doughnut chart can be created using the Chart.Pie and Chart.Doughnut functions. When creating pie or doughnut charts, it is usually desirable to provide both labels and values.
